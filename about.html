About Cakes Dat Rock

Cakes Dat Rock is your go-to bakery for freshly made cakes, pastries, and finger foods like samosas, spring rolls, puff-puff, and meat pies. We also offer in-person training in Abuja and Canada for aspiring bakers!

Our bakery is based in Abuja, Nigeria, and we are excited to soon open a store in Moncton or Dieppe, Canada. We pride ourselves on quality, freshness, and traditional Nigerian flavors.
